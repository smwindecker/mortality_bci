The data that comes with this repository include:

`BCI_traits_20101220.csv` - a dataframe of mean (and SE) wood density estimates for the 203 species used in this study.
`dbh_error_data.rdata` - a dataframe containing repeat measurements for 1,562 individual trees at BCI, for the purpose of quantifying measurement error.

BCI demography data and BCI canopy data are downloaded as part of the remake workflow from their respective published databases (see README.md).
